# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Owners of local Italian service businesses (dentists, gyms, residential/care facilities, personal trainers, restaurants) in and around Torino and Benevento — non-technical, skeptical of agency sales pitches, often with no site or an outdated one. They land here from a referral or a WhatsApp share, on mobile, deciding in seconds whether to keep scrolling or bounce. The job to be done: judge in one visit whether this freelancer can be trusted to build something that brings them real customers, at zero financial risk, then start a conversation on WhatsApp.

## Product Purpose

Della Websites is a solo web-design practice (Massimiliano, 19, Torino & Benevento) that builds fast, conversion-focused one-page sites for local businesses on a "free build, pay only if you go live" model (~€35/month after). This site is the practice's own storefront: it must prove the craft it sells. Success = a visitor believes a real, capable person is behind it and reaches out on WhatsApp or via the contact form.

## Brand Personality

Premium, confident, understated. Studio-grade craft, not startup-flashy — the near-black canvas with brand teal and a new brass accent should read as a deliberate, restrained design decision, not a template. Personal trust (a named 19-year-old builder who signs his own work) is carried through copy and a signed note, not through folksy/cozy visual warmth.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS/startup landing page template: no gradient text, no eyebrow-label-above-every-section, no identical icon+heading card grids, no hero-metric clichés.
- The previous "paper/terracotta" warm-artisan direction this replaces — this is a deliberate reversal away from that cozy, cream-background feel toward a darker, more editorial, studio-grade register.

## Design Principles

- Prove the craft, don't just claim it — every animation and layout choice on this page is itself the sales pitch for the service being sold.
- One accent does the talking — teal (existing brand) and brass (new) are used deliberately and sparingly, not as decoration.
- WhatsApp-first, friction-free — the contact path (WhatsApp CTA, magnetic button, contact form) stays exactly as effective and unobstructed as it is today; this is a visual/motion layer change only.
- Real work over invented work — case studies and service copy reflect actual clients and the actual free-build/monthly pricing model, never placeholder content.
- Motion with intent, not decoration — word-reveal, scroll-triggered cards, magnetic CTA, morphing cursor all exist to reinforce the "this person builds premium, considered sites" claim, gated behind prefers-reduced-motion and pointer:fine.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard WCAG AA: body text ≥4.5:1 contrast against the near-black background, large/display text ≥3:1, full keyboard navigation, semantic HTML landmarks. `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` must disable all decorative motion (word reveals, scroll reveals, cursor, magnetic button, underline draw) in favor of an instant/crossfade state. Custom cursor and magnetic-button effects must stay scoped to `pointer: fine` devices only — never engage on touch.
